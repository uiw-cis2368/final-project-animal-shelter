About Us

Finding homes for every animal because no one deserves to be left behind

The Seaside Pet Rescue provides care, comfort and compassion to animals in need while engaging the hearts, hands and minds of our community to bring about an end to the killing of abandoned and orphaned shelter animals.

We shelter, medically treat, and rehabilitate thousands of animals every year. Many of the animals in our care have been abused, injured, surrendered by their owners, or found as strays. As a no-kill organization, every treatable pet in our care stays in our care until they find a home or necessary placement with another no-kill organization. No pet in our care is ever euthanized to create space.
